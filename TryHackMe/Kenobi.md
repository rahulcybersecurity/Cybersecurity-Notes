# Kenobi - TryHackMe

## Objective

Learn enumeration, service discovery, file access, and Linux privilege escalation techniques.

## Skills Practiced

* Nmap Scanning
* SMB Enumeration
* NFS Enumeration
* File Discovery
* Linux Privilege Escalation

## Enumeration

### Initial Scan

Performed service enumeration to identify open ports and running services.

### SMB Enumeration

Enumerated available SMB shares and discovered useful files.

### NFS Enumeration

Investigated NFS exports and identified accessible directories.

## Key Learning Points

* Enumeration is the most important phase.
* Misconfigured network services can expose sensitive information.
* Combining small findings often leads to full system compromise.

## Privilege Escalation

Practiced Linux privilege escalation techniques and learned how service misconfigurations can be abused.

## Tools Used

* Nmap
* SMB Enumeration Tools
* NFS Utilities

## What I Learned

This room reinforced the importance of thorough enumeration and demonstrated how multiple low-severity findings can be chained together to gain higher privileges.
